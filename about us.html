About the Owner

Product Quality

Our chicken is tender, healthy, and extremely fresh. We use 100% vegetarian feed for our poultry, which keeps them naturally healthy.

No hormones, steroids, or harmful chemicals are used. We follow top-level hygiene and bio-security measures to keep our chickens safe.

We ensure very low disease risk and maintain top-notch quality in every batch.
